 # 🎯 Build Your Year

**Draw your goals. Fill the board. Make this year count.**

Build Your Year is an interactive goal-setting experience that transforms your ambitions into a **playable bingo board**. Instead of writing a traditional resolution list, users visually create their goals directly inside each square by **drawing, doodling, or adding stickers**.

As goals are completed, users stamp the square with a **“Did It”** stamp and watch their progress build across the board. Completing a row, column, or diagonal triggers a satisfying **BINGO moment** 🎉 that motivates users to keep going.

The board automatically updates to the **current year**, making it a timeless tool you can return to every year.

---

# 🧠 Concept

Most resolution lists fail because they are static and easy to ignore.

**Build Your Year turns goal-setting into a visual game.**

Instead of typing goals, users **draw them**, making the experience more personal and memorable. The bingo format introduces milestones and celebration moments that keep users engaged throughout the year.

The result is a **creative and motivating way to track progress.**

---

# 🕹️ How It Works

### 1️⃣ Choose Your Grid
Pick a board size from **3×3 to 6×6**.

### 2️⃣ Draw Your Goals ✏️ 
Each square in the bingo grid acts as a **mini canvas** where users can:

- doodling 🎨  
- adding stickers 🧩  

Save each square to build your personalized board.

### 3️⃣ Click Ready 🚀
Enter **Ready Mode** once the board is complete.

### 4️⃣ Track Progress
Click squares to stamp **Did It** and log the completion date.

Click again to undo if needed.

### 5️⃣ Achieve BINGO
Complete rows, columns, or diagonals to trigger celebration moments 🎉.

### 6️⃣ Export Your Board 📸
Download your board as an image to share or archive.

# 🛠 Built With

This project was originally built using **Figma Make** for the **Figma Makeathon**, where the concept and interactive prototype were created.

After the event, the project was rewritten and coded into an updated standalone version once the professional Figma Make plan expired.,

Current implementation includes:

- Improved drawing system for smoother doodling  
- Multiple grid size options (3×3 - 6×6)  
- Clear grid option to quickly reset the board
